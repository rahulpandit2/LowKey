# 1. Introduction

LowKey is a private-first platform built for thoughtful sharing and real conversations. These policies explain how the platform works, how we protect users, and what behaviour is allowed.

By using LowKey, you agree to follow these policies.

If you do not agree, you should not use the platform.

---

# 2. Terms of Use

You agree that:

* You are at least 13 years old (or the legal minimum age in your country).
* You will not use LowKey for illegal activities.
* You will not attempt to hack, disrupt, or damage the platform.
* You will not create fake accounts to deceive others.
* You are responsible for the content you post.

We may suspend or remove accounts that break these rules.

---

# 3. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important.

We collect only the data necessary to operate the platform.

## Information We Collect

When you create an account:

* Name or username
* Email address
* Password (encrypted)
* Date of birth (if required)

When you use the platform:

* Posts
* Comments
* Reactions
* Messages (if messaging exists)
* Reports you submit

Technical data:

* IP address
* Device type
* Browser type
* Login timestamps

## What We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers.

We do not share your private information publicly.

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

* Provide platform features
* Improve security
* Prevent abuse
* Improve performance
* Send important service updates

We do not use your data to manipulate your behaviour.

---

# 4. Data Usage Policy

Your data is used only for platform operations.

We may use anonymised data for:

* Platform improvement
* Research on community trends
* Security improvements

Anonymised means:

* No names
* No emails
* No personal identifiers

We never publish private user data.

---

# 5. Cookie Policy

We use cookies for:

* Login sessions
* Security
* Basic analytics
* User preferences

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

Users may disable cookies, but some features may stop working.

---

# 6. Community Guidelines

LowKey is designed for respectful conversation.

You must:

* Speak respectfully
* Disagree without attacking
* Avoid personal insults
* Avoid harassment
* Avoid spam

This is not a performance platform. It is a thoughtful space.

---

# 7. Content Policy

Allowed content:

* Personal stories
* Questions
* Educational content
* Creative writing
* Constructive discussions

Not allowed:

* Threats of violence
* Harassment
* Hate speech
* Sexual exploitation
* Non-consensual explicit content
* Promotion of illegal activity
* Fraud or scams

We may remove content that violates these rules.

---

# 8. Moderation Policy

LowKey uses:

* Automated systems to detect harmful content
* Human moderators to review reports
* Escalation review for serious cases

Moderators review:

* Reported posts
* Suspicious behaviour
* Policy violations

Moderation decisions are logged internally.

Moderators do not publicly shame users.

---

# 9. Reporting & Appeals Policy

Users can report:

* Posts
* Comments
* Profiles
* Messages

Reports are reviewed confidentially.

If your content is removed, you may appeal.

Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator when possible.

Repeated abuse of the reporting system may result in penalties.

---

# 10. Account Policy

Each user may have one primary account.

Creating multiple accounts to:

* Avoid suspension
* Manipulate discussions
* Harass others

is not allowed.

Usernames that impersonate:

* Admin
* Support
* Moderators
* Brand accounts

are prohibited.

---

# 11. Identity Verification Policy

Verified Profile means:
The user has submitted a valid government ID.

Verification confirms identity only.

It does not guarantee character, behaviour, or expertise.

Verification badges may be removed if fraud is detected.

---

# 12. Professional Verification Policy

Example: Certified Therapist badge.

To receive this badge:

* The user must submit a valid professional licence.
* The licence must be verified manually.

This badge means:
The user holds a valid licence.

It does NOT mean:

* We judge their quality.
* We endorse them.
* We recommend them.

We only confirm licence authenticity.

---

# 13. Points, Rewards & Badges Policy

Points are given for:

* Helpful posts
* Constructive feedback
* Community contribution

Points are capped monthly to prevent abuse.

Points do not:

* Determine influence
* Affect content visibility
* Create leaderboards

Badges recognise contribution, not popularity.

Internal badges (Dev, Founder, Moderator) cannot be requested by users.

---

# 14. Intellectual Property Policy

Users own the content they create.

By posting on LowKey, you grant us a licence to:

* Display the content
* Store the content
* Moderate the content

You may not post content you do not own or have permission to use.

---

# 15. Copyright & DMCA Policy

If someone posts your copyrighted content:

You may submit a takedown request including:

* Proof of ownership
* URL of infringing content
* Legal declaration

We will review and remove if valid.

False claims may result in penalties.

---

# 16. Anti-Harassment Policy

Harassment includes:

* Repeated unwanted contact
* Threats
* Targeted insults
* Doxxing (sharing private info)

Zero tolerance for:

* Stalking
* Threats of violence

Immediate suspension may occur.

---

# 17. Hate Speech & Extremism Policy

We do not allow:

* Hate speech against protected groups
* Promotion of extremist organisations
* Dehumanising language

Protected characteristics include:

* Race
* Religion
* Gender
* Sexual orientation
* Disability
* Nationality

---

# 18. Misinformation Policy

We may remove content that:

* Spreads harmful false information
* Encourages dangerous actions
* Misleads users intentionally

We may add warnings to sensitive discussions.

---

# 19. Mental Health & Crisis Policy

LowKey is not a medical service.

If a user expresses immediate self-harm risk:

* Moderators may share crisis helpline information.
* Emergency services may be contacted where legally required.

We provide support resources but not therapy.

---

# 20. Child Safety Policy

We strictly prohibit:

* Child sexual exploitation
* Grooming
* Harmful contact with minors

Such content is:

* Immediately removed
* Reported to authorities where required

---

# 21. Safety & Law Enforcement Requests

We may share user data only if:

* Required by valid legal order
* Required by court request
* Required by applicable law

We do not voluntarily share user data.

---

# 22. Security Policy

We use:

* Encryption for passwords
* Secure servers
* Access controls
* Internal logging

Admin access is restricted and monitored.

---

# 23. Data Retention & Deletion Policy

We retain data only as long as necessary.

Deleted accounts:

* Are hidden immediately
* Permanently erased after 30 days

Legal obligations may require longer retention in some cases.

---

# 24. Account Deletion & Recovery Policy

Users may request deletion.

Deletion process:

* Account disabled immediately
* 30-day recovery window
* Permanent deletion after 30 days

Users may request account recovery during the window.

After permanent deletion, recovery is not possible.

These providers must comply with data protection standards.

We are not responsible for third-party external links.
